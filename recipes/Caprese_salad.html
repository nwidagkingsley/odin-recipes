Caprese Salad

Description

Caprese Salad is a simple and refreshing Italian salad made with ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and seasoned with salt and black pepper.

Ingredient

  • 3 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 250g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving platter, alternating them and overlapping slightly.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices
  3. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the Caprese Salad immediately as a light appetizer or side dish.

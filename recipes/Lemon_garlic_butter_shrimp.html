Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp

Description

This dish features succulent shrimp cooked in a flavorful lemon garlic butter sauce, perfect for a quick and delicious meal.

Ingredient

  • 500g large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zest grated
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  2. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes if using. Cook the shrimp until they turn pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  3. Stir in the lemon juice and grated zest, coating the shrimp evenly with the lemon garlic butter sauce
  4. Remove the skillet from heat and sprinkle chopped parsley over the shrimp.
  5. Serve the Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp immediately as a light appetizer or side dish.
